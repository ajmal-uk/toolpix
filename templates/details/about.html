About Tool Pix

Empowering Developers with AI-Powered Coding Tools

Tool Pix, created by UTHAKKAN (UK) and founded by Ajmal U K, is a free platform offering AI-driven compilers and coding tools for Python, Java, C++, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, HTML, and more. Our mission is to make coding accessible, efficient, and innovative for developers worldwide.
